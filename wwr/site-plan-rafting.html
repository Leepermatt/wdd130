Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to increase the sales for the rating company by helping drive awareness of the company. I would like to increase sales by utilizing the website. It should provide information about the trips we offer as well as a way to compare pricing and book appointments.

Audience

The audience that will be using the site will be the outdoor type. They will enjoy spending time outdoors and this website should help them understand what services we offer as well as how to book a trip. They should be able to access the site through a web browser as well as mobile
